MIT Beyond Food Bootcamp
In September 2017 I attended the Beyond Food cohort of the MIT Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp program.

Acceptance rate was 9%, and as the only cohort member still in college, being able to work alongside CEOs and managers from industry was quite an enriching experience.

Top skills and knowledge gained:
  • Early venture launching
  • Product design, value proposition development, LTV/COCA calculation
  • Customer interviewing (Primary Market Research)
  • Investor-pitching
