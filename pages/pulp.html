[pulp]
[pulp] is a social enterprise that designs and builds school chairs out of upcycled cardboard for low-resource classrooms around the world. We had a partnership with the You Have It In You Foundation to build our chairs according to their needs. They run a learning centre in Zimbabwe for students from low-income families.

Top skills and knowledge gained:
  • CAE simulation, specifically with COMSOL Multiphysics and MATLAB
  • Handtool use (ex. wrenching)
  • Materials science: chemical etching, RRR measurement, superconductor science
