Changenuity.weebly.com

Link

Changenuity is a startup I co-founded; it is a web platform that connects impact-seeking people with impact-creating opportunities.

Using Weebly's site builder, I produced the bulk of our pages and their layouts, adding customized CSS styling where appropriate. I also edited our meta tags in order to modify our website's preview on Facebook.

Our design philosophy for the website is to design a a clean, attractive, and easily-understood interface. We were able to validate this during our MVP (minimum viable product) launch period, in which time we reached over 2000 visitors per week

I am currently learning Python and Django to build a user system for Changenuity.

North Surrey Model United Nations

Pictured is a website I designed with a friend for a Model United Nations (MUN) conference that I organized.

At the time, we used only Weebly's site builder, and not any HTML/CSS. Building the website was therefore a pure design task. I learned how to use colour schemes and page layouts in order to communicate information in a way that was both clear and aesthetically-pleasing.

As for a fun fact: our conference donated its profits to the United Nations itself, something that had not been done before!
