Project Summary

This was a pilot project where I explored how to develop a powerless vertical farming (VF) unit. The goal was to alleviate rural poverty in developing countries.

Combining simulations with literature research, I presented a feasibility study at the 2016 Multidisciplinary Undergraduate Research Conference (MURC).

The Result

Powerless vertical farms show technical promise, but would have difficulty being adopted by their intended users. Policy and market dynamics are larger influences than technology on poverty.

Until socio-economic conditions improve, these farms would be a major investment risk for adopters.
